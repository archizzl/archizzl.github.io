radio show episodes

for my first three years of college, i hosted a weekly radio show called "Porpoises LIVE!" and missed maybe two episodes, tops. the title originated from myself and my cohosts' desires to theme each episode's music around a certain action, or purpose. One coughing pronunciation and a few seconds of giggling later, we agreed: it would be really funny if we pronounced purpose as porpoise. A star was born.

i had a lot more time on my hands back then (two wristwatches? are you kidding me?), so i placed a lot of importance upon the graphics i would make to avertise each episode across smatterings of social media posts.

feel free to tune into the station from here, or wherever you are.
